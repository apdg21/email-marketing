📋 1. Interview Q&A + Core Flows OmniSend · multi‑brand
1. Multiple Shopify brands in OmniSend — how do you keep distinct voices?
“In my previous role, I managed three DTC brands using OmniSend.” I maintained voice by creating separate content style guides + swipe files. Example: Brand A witty, Brand B educational. Built separate flow templates and campaign calendars inside OmniSend.
2. Describe auditing an existing flow (abandoned cart / welcome) & optimizing it.
“For an abandoned cart flow, conversion rate was below benchmark.” High drop-off after email 1. A/B tested: control vs variant with 10% discount. Variant improved conversion 15% and revenue per recipient 20%.
3. Process for a seasonal campaign – from concept to post‑analysis, driving revenue.
“Start with promo calendar, align on offer. Build campaign stack in OmniSend: teaser, main launch, last‑chance. Segment by past purchase. After: click‑to‑conversion, revenue per send vs previous period.”

🎭 “Ugly but high‑revenue” flow dilemma

Risk vs reward: never replace a winner – A/B test. Keep “ugly/good” as Control vs “clean/new” as Challenger.

Hierarchy of impact: Offer → Subject → Layout → Copy.

Interview: “If a flow performs well despite poor structure, I’d A/B test a cleaner version against it.”

🏛️ Stability

Don't touch high‑revenue flows without reason.

🤝 Trust

Campaigns build relationship, not just sell.

📈 Growth

More subscribers = more flow revenue.
