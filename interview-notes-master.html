Interview Q&A — technical & fit

must know
1. Multiple Shopify brands in OmniSend — distinct voices?
“Managed three DTC brands using OmniSend. Separate style guides + flow templates.”
2. Auditing an existing flow (abandoned cart) & optimisation?
“For abandoned cart, high drop-off after email1. A/B tested discount variant – lifted conversion 15%.”
3. Seasonal campaign – concept to post‑analysis?
“Promo calendar, OmniSend campaign stack: teaser, launch, last‑chance. Analyse revenue per send.”
4. Flagged issue with creative/EST hours?
“Noticed mobile render issue, huddled with designer, fixed assets.”
5. OmniSend Shopify integration – product recommender, discount codes?
“Use Product Recommender blocks, unique discount codes in Welcome flows.”
6. High open rate but revenue dip – troubleshooting?
“Check CTR first → if low, CTA; if high, landing page friction.”
7. SMS or deliverability experience?
“Integrated SMS into OmniSend flows (back in stock). sunset unengaged profiles.”

🧠Strategy: emphasise revenue per send, flow optimisation, segmentation.
