🛡️ conservative

retention & trust

When market feels quiet or data signals softness. Focus on engagement, low pressure, brand building.

Pitch: “If the market is quiet, I switch to my retention-heavy Strategy C – protect list health, keep trust high.”
⚖️ balanced

core revenue + growth

Standard month: mix of acquisition campaigns, storytelling, and standard flows.

Pitch: “I build three paths based on past 90 days. Balanced is my go-to – steady campaigns, list growth, flow optimization.”
🚀 aggressive

scale & conversion

If early campaign overperforms, lean into discount angles, urgency, last‑chance pushes.

Pitch: “If a campaign pops, I pivot to Strategy A – amplify winning offers, stack more frequent sends, capture momentum.”

🎙️ interview pitch “I don’t just plan a single path. I build three sets of strategies based on 90‑day data. This lets me pivot instantly — aggressive if momentum hits, conservative if the market feels quiet. Keeps revenue stable and my thoughts organized.”

⏱️ 48‑hour pulse check

Industry gold standard – ~95% of email revenue arrives within 48h. I analyse post-send data immediately to optimize the very next send.

“I have a strict 48‑hour analysis window. If data dips, I don’t wait until month end – I use that intel to tweak the next campaign.”

📅 YoY benchmarking

Comparing January to December is unfair. I always look at Year‑over‑Year (e.g., Jan 2026 vs Jan 2025) to separate seasonal trends from deliverability issues.

📊 seasonal trend → trust content ⚠️ deliverability drop → re‑engagement

🌱 healthy list philosophy

“List growth is more important than a temporary revenue dip.” If we hit revenue but burn the list with high unsubscribes, we lose long term.

🔄 revenue flywheel:

Trust → retention (low unsub) → list growth → more flow triggers → more revenue.

📌 “Engagement over exhaustion” – I don’t overwhelm subscribers, I keep design simple, clean, responsive.

💡 segment refreshing: Sometimes unengaged just need a better subject line or offer. I use OmniSend to wake them up.

🧼 deliverability-first design

Clean, responsive emails with balanced text‑to‑image ratios. I prioritise simple code to keep spam rates low and engagement high.

“Maybe some who were interested got busy and removed — that’s segment refreshing” – they might just need a different trigger.

📉 if unsubs spike?

Check YoY first. If trend, trust campaigns. If deliverability, re‑engagement sequence.
