Interview Q&A — technical & fit

must know
1. Multiple Shopify brands in OmniSend — how do you keep distinct voices?
“In my previous role, I managed three DTC brands using OmniSend. I maintained voice by creating separate content style guides + swipe files in our PM system. Example: Brand A (witty/irreverent) vs Brand B (educational/professional). I built separate flow templates and campaign calendars inside OmniSend to prevent crossover.”
2. Describe auditing an existing flow (abandoned cart / welcome) & optimizing it.
“For an abandoned cart flow, conversion rate was below benchmark. I saw high drop-off after email 1. A/B tested: control 'we saved your cart' vs variant with 10% discount. Variant improved conversion 15% and revenue per recipient 20%.”
🔍 metrics: open rate, conversion per send, conditional splits (high‑value vs low‑value carts).
3. Process for a seasonal campaign – from concept to post‑analysis, driving revenue.
“Start with promo calendar, align on offer. Build campaign stack in OmniSend: teaser, main launch, last‑chance. Segment by past purchase behavior. After campaign: analyse click‑to‑conversion, revenue per send vs previous period.”
→ connect to ROI, not just opens.
4. Tell me about a time you flagged an issue with creative/ops (EST hours).
“Noticed creative assets didn't render on mobile (70% traffic). Hopped on Slack huddle with designer, shared screenshots, discussed mobile‑friendly fix. Revised assets launched with high conversion.”
proactive flagging · ownership
5. OmniSend Shopify integration – product recommender, discount codes, sync?
“I use Product Recommender blocks dynamically (best‑sellers, new arrivals). I prefer 'Unique Discount Code' in Welcome flows to prevent code sharing. Ensure Shopify segments sync seamlessly for win‑back/post‑purchase triggers.”
6. High open rate but revenue dip – troubleshooting process?
“Check Click‑Through Rate first. If CTR low → weak CTA. If CTR high but revenue low → landing page friction or offer clarity. Then test layout or urgency tactic (limited time).”
7. (nice to have) SMS or deliverability experience?
“Yes – integrated SMS into OmniSend flows: 'Back in Stock' and 'Flash Sale'. For deliverability: sunset unengaged profiles, avoid over‑sending to all subscribers, maintain list hygiene.”
