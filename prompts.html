Step-by-Step Guide

How the Workflow Works

A practical walkthrough from first planning session to performance review.

📅
Step 01 · Strategy
🗓 Start of each month
Plan your full month calendar

Open the Strategy Prompt Generator. Set your brand name, target month, goals (revenue, retention, win-back, etc.), send frequency, and audience segments. Paste your last 90 days of campaign data into the metrics field — even a few key numbers like open rates and top products helps significantly.

Copy the generated prompt and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. The AI will return a full email calendar table with: No., Email Name, Date, Promo Start, Promo End, Purpose, Subject Line, Preview Text, Segment, Product Focus, Send Time, and Discount Code — one row per send.

✍️
Step 02 · Copy
🖊 Before each send
Turn each strategy row into full email copy

Open the Copy Prompt Generator. Copy one row from your strategy table and paste it into the row parser — it will split all 12 fields automatically. Set your brand voice, choose a tone (warm, urgent, storytelling, etc.), and select which elements to include.

Copy the prompt and paste it into your AI of choice. You'll receive: 4+ subject line variations, preview text, personalized opening, full body copy, primary CTA, closing, P.S. line, and a detailed hero image prompt for Midjourney, DALL-E, or Adobe Firefly.

🎨
Step 03 · Design
💻 After copy is approved
Build the production HTML email

Open the Design Prompt Generator. Paste the email copy from Step 2, add your hero image URL, and configure the brand settings. Copy the generated prompt and run it through Claude or ChatGPT.

The output is a fully production-ready HTML email — all styles inlined, mobile responsive, table-based layout, and matching the Boho Blanket brand system. Upload directly into Omnisend or Klaviyo's custom HTML block.

⏳
Step 04 · Wait
⏱ 48–72 hours after send
Wait before checking results

Don't check performance too early. Open rates take 24–48 hours to stabilize as subscribers open across time zones and devices. Revenue attribution needs 48–72 hours for post-click purchases to fully register in your ESP.

Check back on Day 2 or Day 3 for the most accurate read on how a campaign performed.

📊
Step 05 · Analyze
📤 Export CSV from your ESP
Review performance and feed it back into next month

Export your campaign CSV from Omnisend or Klaviyo and upload it to the analytics dashboard. Review: open rate by send day, click rate by campaign type, revenue per send, segment performance, and unsubscribe trends.

Note which subject line styles, send days, and campaign types performed best. Paste these insights into the Strategy Prompt Generator at the start of next month — the AI will use them to build a smarter, more optimized calendar.

Pro Tips

Get the Most from Each Tool

📋
Paste your 90-day data as a table

Copy your campaign export directly from Omnisend or Klaviyo and paste the raw tab-separated data into the Strategy tool's metrics field. The AI reads column headers and values natively — no reformatting needed.

🔁
Reuse the strategy table row-by-row

Your AI-generated strategy table is your source of truth for the month. Copy one row at a time into the Copy Prompt Generator. No need to re-enter brand context every time — it saves between sessions.

🖼️
Use the hero image prompt in Midjourney

The Copy Prompt Generator outputs a detailed hero image brief. Paste it directly into Midjourney with --ar 3:2 --style raw for a clean flat-lay that matches your email's mood.

⚡
A/B test using the subject line variations

The Copy tool generates 4–6 subject line options across different angles. Use Omnisend or Klaviyo's A/B split feature to test two at a time. Note the winner in your next month's 90-day data paste to train future strategy prompts.

⏰
Check analytics on Day 2, not Day 1

Resisting the urge to check immediately leads to better decisions. Open rates on Day 1 are often 15–20% lower than final totals. Wait 48 hours before drawing conclusions or deciding whether to resend to non-openers.

🔄
The loop: data → strategy → copy → design

This workflow compounds over time. Each month's analytics feed the next month's strategy, which produces better copy, which drives better results. After 3 months, the AI has enough context to build highly personalized, data-optimized calendars.
