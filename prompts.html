Step-by-Step Guide

How the Workflow Works

A practical walkthrough from first planning session to performance review.

📅
Step 01 · Strategy
🗓 Start of each month
Plan your full month calendar

Open the Strategy Prompt Generator. Set your brand name, target month, goals, send frequency, and audience segments. Paste your last 90 days of campaign data — even a few key numbers like open rates and top products makes a big difference.

Copy the generated prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. The AI returns a full email calendar table with all 12 columns: No., Email Name, Date, Promo Start, Promo End, Purpose, Subject Line, Preview Text, Segment, Product Focus, Send Time, and Discount Code.

✍️
Step 02 · Copy
🖊 Before each send
Turn each strategy row into full email copy

Open the Copy Prompt Generator. Copy one row from your strategy table and paste it into the row parser — it splits all 12 fields automatically. Pick a tone, choose which elements to include, then copy the prompt into your AI of choice.

You'll receive: 4+ subject line variations, preview text, personalized opening, full body copy, primary CTA, closing, P.S. line, and a detailed hero image prompt for Midjourney, DALL-E, or Adobe Firefly.

🎨
Step 03 · Design
💻 After copy is approved
Build the production HTML email

Open the Design Prompt Generator. Paste the email copy, add the hero image URL, configure brand settings, then run the prompt through Claude or ChatGPT.

The output is a fully production-ready HTML email — all styles inlined, mobile-responsive, table-based layout, matching the Boho Blanket brand system. Upload directly into Omnisend or Klaviyo's custom HTML block.

🚀
Schedule & Send
Schedule and publish in your ESP

Upload the HTML into Omnisend or Klaviyo, set the send time from your strategy table, choose your segment, and schedule. Then — step away. Don't check the stats yet.

⏳
Wait
Wait 48–72 hours before reviewing

Open rates take 24–48 hours to stabilize across time zones and devices. Revenue attribution needs 48–72 hours for post-click purchases to fully register. Checking on Day 1 gives you a distorted picture — wait until Day 2 or Day 3 for an accurate read.

📊
Step 04 · Analyze
📤 48–72 hours after send
Analyze the campaign with AI

Open the Performance Analysis Prompt Generator. Paste your campaign metrics row from the ESP export — or fill in manually. Add 2–5 recent campaigns for benchmarking if available (leave blank if not — the AI skips comparisons gracefully).

Copy the prompt into your AI of choice. You'll get: a performance summary, metric-by-metric breakdown with star ratings, funnel role identification, a key insight, benchmark comparison (if data provided), and concrete next-step recommendations for what to send in the next 24–72 hours.

🔄
Loop · Next Month
📋 Feed insights back into Step 01
Feed analysis back into next month's strategy

Copy the AI's key insights from Step 04 and paste them into the Strategy Prompt Generator's "Last 90-day data" field next month. The AI will use your actual performance patterns — best send days, subject line styles that worked, top-converting campaign types — to build a smarter, more optimized calendar every month.

Pro Tips

Get the Most from Each Tool

📋
Paste your 90-day data as a table

Copy your campaign export directly from Omnisend or Klaviyo and paste the raw tab-separated data into the Strategy tool's metrics field. The AI reads column headers and values natively — no reformatting needed.

🔁
Reuse the strategy table row-by-row

Your AI-generated strategy table is your source of truth for the month. Copy one row at a time into the Copy Prompt Generator. The campaign context flows through automatically.

🖼️
Use the hero image prompt in Midjourney

The Copy tool outputs a detailed hero image brief. Paste it into Midjourney with --ar 3:2 --style raw for a clean flat-lay that matches your email's mood.

⚡
A/B test using the subject line variations

The Copy tool generates 4–6 subject line options across different angles. Use Omnisend or Klaviyo's A/B feature to test two at a time. Log the winner in your next month's 90-day data paste.

⏰
Check analytics on Day 2, not Day 1

Open rates on Day 1 are often 15–20% lower than final totals. Wait 48 hours before drawing conclusions or deciding whether to resend to non-openers. The Performance Analysis tool reminds you of this automatically.

🧠
Add previous campaigns to the analysis prompt

The Performance Analysis tool has a benchmark table — add 3–5 recent campaigns with their metrics. The AI will rank the current send, spot patterns, and give sharper "what to do next" recommendations when it has context to compare against.

📌
The "what to do next" insight is the most valuable

After each campaign analysis, the AI gives 2–3 next-step options with timing and expected revenue. This is the output that closes the loop — it tells you whether to send an SMS follow-up, a last-chance email, or to reset and nurture.

🔄
The workflow compounds over time

Each month's analysis feeds the next month's strategy, which produces better copy, which drives better results. After 3 months, the AI has real performance data to build highly optimized, personalized calendars.
